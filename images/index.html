Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike?

Prioritize safety by obeying road rules, including traffic signals and laws. Adopt a mindset of "driving" rather than just "riding" your bike for enhanced focus and legal compliance.

How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one?

Check your helmet for wear, focusing on cracks, dents, padding condition, and strap integrity. Replace if it's over 5 years old to maintain safety standards.

My bike has been in storage is it safe to ride?

Perform a thorough safety check on your bike, including tires, brakes, and all components, after storage. Take a brief test ride to confirm everything is working before extended riding.

What rules should I follow when riding my bike?

When riding your bike, always obey traffic laws, wear a helmet, signal your intentions, and stay aware of your surroundings to ensure safety for yourself and others.
